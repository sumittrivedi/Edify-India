    What is education?


    Is it book-learning?
    Is it diverse knowledge?
    Is it scoring good marks?
    Is it holding good rank in an institution?
    Is is qualifying Competitive examinations?

    No, This is not education.

    From a research , Harvard University found that when a person gets a job, 85% of the time it is because of their attitude, and only 15% of the time because of how smart they are and how many facts and figures they know. Surprisingly, almost 100% of education rupees go to teach facts and figures which account for only 15% of success in work!

    Swami Vivekananda's views on education

    The training by which the current and expression of will are brought under control and become fruitful is called education. Education is not filling the mind with a lot of facts. Perfecting the instrument and getting complete mastery of my own mind.We want that education by which character is formed,strength of mind is increased,the intellect is expanded and by which one can stand on one's own feet
